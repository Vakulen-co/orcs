What is Orcs Town?

Orcs Town is a collection of 10,000 generative hand-drawn NFT orcs building a town on the Ethereum network.

They 'll be playable characters in our upcoming multiplayer online game called “Orcs Town”. Through raiding, stacking and renting, Holders will accumulate both in-game $ORCS tokens for in-game activity and monthly $DND profit for marketplace purchases.

What the purpose of Dungeons?

Dungeons is a fancy name for Land Plots. It’s a collection of 20,000 unique plots scatter across Orcs Town metaverse. Holders will be able to rent Dungeons to Game Masters in order to get monthly $DND profit from the non-holder pool.

Dungeons can be merged, if certain criteria will be met:

  • Dungeons needs to have same biome
  • Dungeonsneeds to have same tier (T1 Lava + T1 Lava = T2 Lava)

What is the Artifacts and Building Materials?

During the "Era of Building," players who hold Orcs will receive artifacts, and players who hold dungeons will receive building materials. They will act as a multiplier for DnD and Orcs tokens generation

Gameplay

You have been chosen by Old Orc to save the world from evil. You must unite your team and fight against the horde of creatures and demons that are trying to keep you away from your goal.

Roll a Dice

You will be divided into teams of 4-6 players, depending on the storyline. Each player will get one chance to roll a dice. The difficulty will be determined by Old Orc (Game Master AI). If a player throws a higher or equal number, his team can go through an obstacle in their way. If not, well… face consensus!

Additional Turns

If an Orc Holder fails to overcome an obstacle, he must pay $DND for each additional turn he wants to take. Non-holders must pay $DND for each turn they take. The amount needed increases with every turn taken after the first one!

Secret Rooms

Every main storyline will have secret rooms, where you can find high-tier artifacts. Secret rooms are the most dangerous places in the game !!! If a player will die during the secret room storylcine, all artifacts which have been gathered will be burned forever!!!!
